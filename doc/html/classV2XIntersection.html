Detailed Description

A representation of an intersection in the context of V2XFramework.

In the context of V2X communications, the Road Side Units (RSU) are entities that get information from the intersection(s) they are connected to and broadcast them to the near vehicles. This class translates information from the Aimsun world to the V2X world. This done through the filling of common structures, that are defined by the ETSI standard.

The intersection is strictly linked to a node, and can obtain information from it.

Reasoning in terms of the framework, the intersections are connections between V2XGenericLane objects.
